## About / full description (EN)
Welcome to Skullchef: The Wooden Soup — a whimsical idle clicker in a cozy fantasy kitchen.

Tap to stir the cauldron and watch your soup bubble to life. Spend earnings in the shop to summon helpers, grow ingredients on your farm, unlock collectible character skins, and ascend via a deep prestige system. Daily missions, achievements, global leaderboards, offline rewards, and satisfying sound effects.

Play in English or Spanish. By SmallCreek.
